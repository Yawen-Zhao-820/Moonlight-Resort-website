    Bungee Jumping from the Craters on the Moon

  • Bungee Jumping on the Moon:The most exciting and popular activity!

    Want to experience something exciting and new?
    Try bungee jumping from the top of the craters on the Moon!
    Slower than bungee jumping on the Earth but still fun and thrilling.
    Enjoy the magnificent view of the universe when falling down from the top of the crater!

    Caution:
    1. Do not participate if you have heart disease or high blood pressure.
    2. Your security will be guarded by professional staff.
    3. You can bungee jump from your favorite crater of your choice.
    4. Staff can be contacted at any time during the bungee jumping by the intercom in the space suit.
