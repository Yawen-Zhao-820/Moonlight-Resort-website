  • The 1st Day

    Arrival

  • The first day of your vacation:
    Relax and have fun!

    Why not have a good rest?
    Enjoy the facilities in the resort!

    Activities:
    1. Go to Crater Model Studio and make a clay model by yourself.
    2. Go to Moonlight Restaurant and have a nice meal.
    3. Have a cup of coffee in Gravity Free Cafe.
    4. Rest in the Dexluxe Ocean View Suite.

  • The 2nd Day

    Fly to the Moon

  • Stuff drive you to the Moon!

    Start to enjoy your vacation on the Moon!
    Do not participate if you have heart disease or high blood pressure.

    Activities:
    1. Wear a space suit and enjoy walking on the moon with low gravity.
    2. Wander around, find your favorite crater and remember its' name.
    3. Rest in the Deluxe Moon Crater Suite.
    4. Learn to drive a car on the Moon with instructions.

  • The 3rd Day

    Car racing on the Moon

  • Exciting car racing on the Moon!

    Race and drift!
    Do not participate if you have heart disease or high blood pressure.

    Activities
    1. Enjoy your car racing on the surface of the Moon.
    2. Having a great meal in Moonlight Restaurant.
    3. Have a good rest in the Deluxe Moon Crater Suite and prepare for tommorow's bungee jumping!

  • The 4th Day

    Bungee jumping!

  • Bungee jumping from the top of your favorite crater!

    The most popular and exciting activity of Moonlight Resort.
    Don't forget to rent a camera from the service center.
    Do not participate if you have heart disease or high blood pressure.

    Activities
    1. Enjoy bungee jumping from the top of your favorite crater!
    2. Ask the stuff to fly you to the perfect location for selfies!
    3. Go to Moonlight Handicraft Shop to get some handmade souvenirs.
    4. Have a good rest in the Deluxe Moon Crater Suite and prepare to come back Earth.
